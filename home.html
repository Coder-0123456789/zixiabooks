二十多年来，我的工作始终围绕一个核心问题展开：学习者如何才能最有效地习得第一语言和第二语言？

作为一名持有教师资格证的汉语教师、应用语言学研究员和儿童读写书籍作者，我通过教学实践、学术研究和书籍写作来探索这个问题。多年来，这些经历使我形成了诸多独特的见解。

在这个网站上，我展示了我的主要工作和项目。您可以在这里找到我撰写的书籍、发表的学术文章，以及我旅行中的感想和笔记。

Home

For more than two decades, my work has centered on one core question: How do learners most effectively acquire a first and a second language?

As a certified Mandarin teacher, an applied linguistics researcher, and an author of children’s literacy books, I explore this question through teaching practice, academic research, and book writing. Over the years, these experiences have led me to develop a number of unique insights.

On this website, I present my main work and projects. Here you can find the books I have written, the academic articles I have published, and reflections and notes from my travels.
